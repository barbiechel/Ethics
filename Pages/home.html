ETHICS COMMITTEE

Online Micro Sellers Marketing Cooperative

FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES

An ETHICS Committee shall be composed of three (3) members to be appointed by the Board of Directors.

Within ten (10) days after their appointment, they shall elect from among themselves a Chairperson, Vice-Chairperson and Secretary who shall serve for a term of one (1) year or until their successors shall have been appointed and qualified without prejudice to their re appointment
