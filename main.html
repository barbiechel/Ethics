OMSMC CODE OF ETHICAL STANDARDS

This code refers to the framework from which the Online Micro Sellers Marketing Cooperative (OMSMC) Board of Directors, Committee Members, Other Key Officers and Management Team can be inspired to work collectively in building an effective, efficient, and sustainable cooperative

The Code of Ethical Standards (also called Code of Ethics and Conducts) is a statement of the ethical principles, values and behaviors expected of the Board of Directors, Committee Members, Key Officers, Management Team and Members of the Cooperative. It is designed to guide them in their dealings with one another, their representatives, and others they come in contact within the course of performing their duties and responsibilities. It puts forward a set of general principles rather than detailed prescriptions
